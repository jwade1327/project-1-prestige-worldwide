Let us Host!

You just heard your favorite band is going on tour, the excitement starts! The first thing you do is to checkout and see where they are going to play. As quick and your excitment starts it is gone in a blink of an eye because they aren't touring anywhere near where you live.

We have all experienced the highs and lows of wanting to see the bands you love, but not being about to see them because of the venue location. You never know when a band's tour will be their last. This is where we come in. We love music just as much as you so let us help make sure you won't miss out again.

Make it Happen

Find a band you want to see? Let's make it happen! All you have to do is make a donation, simple as that. Once your band has met its goal, your donation now becomes your tickets. For example, if you donate $40 that will in turn get you 4 tickets. If your band does not meet the goal then your card will not be charged.

Now, the hard part...which band do you want to see most?

Pick the band you would like to see and leave the rest to Prestige Worldwide! Enter your donation amount.
